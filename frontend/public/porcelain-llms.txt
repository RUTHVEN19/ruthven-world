# PORCELAIN ANDROID — The Manga Machine

> An immersive cryptoart world by Miss AL Simpson, a Scottish cryptoartist, award-winning artist and founder. Neon-lit Roppongi nightlife rendered in manga: porcelain figures, cherubs, showgirls, latex and light. Built with a LoRA trained on the artist's own body of work.

## About
- Artist: Miss AL Simpson — Scottish cryptoartist, AI filmmaker, founder. Her film "Les Drones de la Banlieue" was exhibited at Sotheby's.
- Porcelain Android is one of her worlds. "The Manga Machine" is its central project: still artworks and AI short films of a Tokyo-after-dark district.
- Everything is made and built by the artist: the trained LoRA, the generation and upscaling pipeline, the smart contracts, the minting tools, and the interactive 3D galleries that house the work.

## Collections
- THE MANGA MACHINE - PORCELAIN ANDROIDS (Ethereum): a fixed, permanent 116-piece on-chain archive. Contract 0xE7C62655148B86f8829608378C638a03E73342bA. Royalties to the artist; intellectual property retained by Miss AL Simpson Limited.
